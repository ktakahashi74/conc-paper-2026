Methodology

Seeds are taken by ordinal index (0 and 10) from the paper's shared seed lists — no cherry-picking. All tracks are rendered with conchordal-render: harmonic voices, mono WAV at 48 kHz, no reverb, no mastering, identical chain across all tracks.

These audio demonstrations are qualitative support only. Quantitative claims rest on the paper's simulation statistics (Section 5). The audio is generated by the instrument's native Rhai scenario system, not by replaying simulation event logs.
