E3 metric definitions
C is the 0-1 score passed into ArticulationCore::process as `consonance` (agent.last_consonance_level01()).
C_firstK definition: mean over first K=20 ticks after birth (0..1).
Metabolism update (conceptual):
  E <- E - basal_cost_per_sec * dt
  Attack tick: E <- E - action_cost + recharge_per_attack * C
C is clamped to [0,1] in the metabolism step (defensive), so recharge is continuous.
NoRecharge sets recharge_per_attack=0, so the C-dependent recharge term is removed.
Representative seed is chosen by the median Pearson r of baseline C_firstK vs lifetime; pooled plots concatenate all seeds.
c_level01_birth=first tick value; c_level01_firstk=mean of first K ticks; avg_c_level01_tick=mean over life; c_level01_std_over_life=std over life; avg_c_level01_attack=mean over attack ticks.
